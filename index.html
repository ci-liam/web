Cecilia Miranda Garcés

Media Artist / Writer / Researcher

My artistic and literary practice explores the frictions between human memory and machine memory.

I make no distinction between code and word: both are language. I work through "expanded writing," creating interactive installations, essays on technology, and poetic archives.

MA in New Media (University of Leeds) and Literature (UASB). My recent work investigates how we inhabit trauma and affect within digital interfaces.

Artistic Practice

Memory Flux University Multimedia Art Biennial (BUAM) 2025
Interactive installation combining a digital interface (poetic software) with a physical analog writing component. The work explores the persistence and degradation of memory in the era of infinite storage.
Code Societies School for Poetic Computation, NY (2025)
Research and development of critical code. Exploration of social protocols embedded within network infrastructure.
