About

I am a writer, researcher and creative coder from Quito, Ecuador.

I hold MAs with Distinction in New Media (University of Leeds) and Literature (UASB). My writing has been featured in international anthologies, such as the V Anthology of the NYC Book Fair, and literary journals. My first essay collection, El pulso del código, winner of the 2025 Editorial Municipal de Cuenca Editorial Call, is forthcoming in 2026.

Currently, I am developing research that weaves together formal literary analysis and computational practice.

University Biennial of Multimedia Art (2026)

Participants created a hybrid work that materializes an algorithm using wool, code, and language.

INSTRUCTION: The final word of a verse is the start of the next.

I pick up the earth with my hands.
Earth that comes from the terrace.
Terrace where my friends rest.
Rest on hammocks.
Hammocks we brought from Jipijapa.
Jipijapa, place of my first bollo.
Bollo wrapped in plantain leaf.
Plantain that grows in heat.
Heat that rarely inhabits Quito.
Quito, the earth I step on.
Floor where the earth sleeps.
Earth I pick up with my hands.
Hands that embrace my friends.
Friends tired.
Tired like their hands.
Hands that have worked the earth.
Ana Paula
